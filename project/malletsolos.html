Mallet Solo Videos Description
In this video, Matthew Ho plays a famous piece called the Flight of the Bumblebee. This piece is one of the fastest pieces in the world and is very difficult to play. Look how crazy fast he has to play, really sounds like a bumblebee am I right?
This piece is one of my favorite songs to play. The music has a very melodic tone and has great emotion. Everytime it rains outside, it reminds me of this song. Enjoy this everlasting piece.
In this video, Aaron Trumbore plays a very lively marimba piece. The piece has a very emotional movement and really gave me the feeling. This video suprised me because I rarely see mallet pieces played by J.S. Bach.
This is also one of my favorite marimba pieces and one of my first Four-Mallet piece every learned. This piece is very calm and feels very peaceful. The melody feels like the aftermath after precipitation.

Drum Set Videos Description
In this video, Buddy Rich plays Bugle Call Rag which is very famous. This song has crazy solos, rhythms, insane rudiments, and just flashy moves. This guy gives me the goosebumps because his play style is way too fast. I have no comment. Sadly, Buddy Rich passed awag in 1987.
In this drum cover, Fede Rabaquino plays one of my rock music favorite's American Idiot. This songs has great motion and heavy intensity. In addition, it gives a big pulse and helps drive the motion very powerfully foward.
Dylan Elise, a drum soloists, is one of the best current drummers in the world in my opinion. He is under-rated, but very skilled and swift on the drum set. His style is very free and not really in the section of Jazz or Rock. The style is more international and crazy.
